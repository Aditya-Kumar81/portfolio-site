Building My Portfolio Blog with Hugo

2025-09-19kiteflinty

Why Hugo?#

I wanted a fast, lightweight, and customizable way to showcase my projects, thoughts, and ideas. Hugo stood out because:

  • It’s a static site generator (no databases, no heavy backend).
  • Super fast builds — it can generate thousands of pages in seconds.
  • Markdown-based content, which feels natural for writing blogs.
  • Huge collection of themes that are easy to customize.

Setting Up the Blog#

After installing Hugo, I created my site with:

hugo new site portfolio-blog
